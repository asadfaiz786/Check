    History

    		 (Back To Top)

         Beaconhouse-Informatics has emerged as Pakistan's
    leading network of information technology education
    institutes.With existing branches in Karachi,Lahore,
    Islamabad,Multan,and upcoming branches in other major
    cities of Pakistan,Beaconhouse-Informatics is poised to
    become a major player in the country's IT and software
    institutions.

          The Beaconhouse-Informatics alliance was formed in
    1996 to fill an ever growing void in quality IT education
    in Pakistan.The first branch was established in Lahore in
    April 1996.Beaconhouse-Informatics soon established a third
    branch in Karachi in October 1996.Beaconhouse-Informatics
    Islamabad followed in March 1997,and our Multan centre opened
    in July 1998.Since then a second branch in Karachi has opened
    in Gulshan-e-Iqbal,and Lahore,as well as the Islamabad centre
    have been relocated to bigger and better premises.

         Beaconhouse-Informatics continues to expand in order to
    keep pace with the domestic and international markets' insatiable
    demands for qualified IT personnel.

    Curtin University Of Technology

    		 (Back To Top)

          Curtin University of Technology is a member of the
    Australian Vice Chancellors' Committee and the
    Association of Australian Universities. It has built
    enviable links with industry, government and other
    institutions throughout Asia and the world. Curtin
    University degrees are recognized both nationally and
    internationally.

         Curtin University has recently won national acclaim as one
    of Australia's outstanding universities in the Federal
    Government's Quality Assurance review on Higher Education.
    In this report, the committee judges Curtin as one of
    Australia's best universities for both quality research and
    community service.

    Portsmouth University UK

    		 (Back To Top)

          Established in 1869, the University of Portsmouth is a leading
    UK university with a student population in excess of 16,500
    students. It is a member of the Association of Commonwealth
    Universities (ACU) and is recognized by the Institute of
    Quality Assurance in the UK as a Centre of Excellence based
    on detailed evaluation of their teaching,research and consultancy
    work.Since its inception the University of Portsmouth has maintained
    its commitment to promoting and maintaining excellence in teaching,
    research and scholarship across a wide range of disciplines.

    Punjab University

    		 (Back To Top)

         Established in 1850, the University of Punjab is a leading
    Pakistan university with a student population in excess of 50,500
    students.

         Informatics International is a world leader in providing
    information technology education and training. Apart from
    its strong network in the Asia-Pacific region, Informatics
    also has a strong foothold in Europe through its acquisition
    of NCC Education Services, UK, as well as C-TEC
    (now Informatics C-TEC), UK's premier IT training
    company with 14 centres in London, Manchester,
    Leeds, Glasgow, Edinburgh, and other cities. Informatics
    continues its expansion in Europe and North Africa through
    its regional headquarters in Manchester, UK.

         Through Informatics International, Beaconhouse-Informatics
    is also able to offer its students degrees from approximately
    50 world-class universities. These universities accept credits
    that have been earned at all Informatics institutes worldwide.

         In Pakistan, Beaconhouse-Informatics currently offers two
    (of these 30) quality degrees through to completion, while
    students pursuing the other degrees have to proceed overseas
    for the final year of studies.

         In Pakistan, Informatics continues this ‘tradition of
    excellence’ with its partner, the Beaconhouse School System,
    Pakistan's largest and oldest private school system with
    its own reputation for excellence in the field of education
