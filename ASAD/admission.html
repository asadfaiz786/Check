Admission

  When you decide Informatics is where you want to begin - or continue - your college education,you
can apply using the online application.Freshmen and transfer applications for admission are
due Aug. 1 for the fall semester, or Dec. 1 for the spring semester. If you're a high school senior,
apply for admission during the first semester of your senior year; provide a transcript of all the
courses you've taken so far, plus the ones in progress. If you want to transfer to Informatics,
you should apply four to six months before the start of the semester in which you plan to enroll.
