Students Life

   It is the mission of Student Services to provide students, staff, faculty, parents,
community members, and alumni essential services in order to promote and ensure an effective living
and learning community. These services include housing, program mentoring, counseling, insurance,
career resources, referrals, co-curricular activities, orientation and graduation events.
